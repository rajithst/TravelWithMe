Welcome to Serene Bridge Resort

Welcome to Serene Bridge Resort a resort just one hour drive away from busy Colombo the commercial capital of Sri Lanka. The resort is surrounded by lush green rubber plantations with giving cool breeze to our visitors who are from all over the world to discover this miraculous island.
